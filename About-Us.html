About the Toki Spa

The Toki Spa is an imaginary, made up company for learning purposes whose mission statement is to provide its customers with a unique, international array of massage techniques aimed to relax!

We have an experienced and culturally diverse group of staff, hailing from parts of India, to Thailand Japan, along with those from Turkey and different parts of the United States all under one roof here in New York City, all providing different massage services you won't find anywhere else.

Along with our affordable prices and unique services, we wish to provide you a tingly, yet relaxing experience that focuses on clearing tension and stress from all parts of your body, especially the ones where you'd least expect our techniques to offer its unique relaxation.

Our customers are always right! Here at The Toki Spa, we want you leaving our stores feeling satisfied and endlessly refreshed, ready to tackle the day by the horns. So just....RELAAAAAAAX.
